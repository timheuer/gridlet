# Crucigram — App Store Information

## App Name

Crucigram: Daily Crosswords

## Subtitle

## Description

Challenge yourself with a fresh crossword puzzle every day — or play unlimited puzzles anytime you want.

Crucigram delivers bite-sized 5×5 and 6×6 crossword puzzles you can solve in minutes. Tap to select, type to fill, and check your work as you go. Keep your daily streak alive or practice with unlimited randomly-generated puzzles.

**Daily Puzzle**
A new puzzle every day, generated just for you. Solve it to maintain your streak and track your stats.

**Unlimited Play**
Want more? Play as many puzzles as you like, anytime. Each one is uniquely generated on your device.

**Simple Controls**
• Tap a cell to select it and see the clue
• Tap again to switch between across and down
• Type letters to fill in the grid
• Tap Check to verify your current word

**Track Your Progress**
• Current and longest streaks
• Total puzzles solved
• Average completion time

**Fully Offline**
No internet required. Every puzzle is generated right on your device using advanced on-device intelligence.

**Privacy First**
No accounts. No tracking. No ads. Just puzzles.

## App Review Notes

Crucigram is a crossword puzzle game that works entirely offline. No account, permissions, or network connection required.

**How to Test:**

1. Launch app → 5-page onboarding appears (can skip or tap through)
2. Tap "Daily Puzzle" or "Unlimited" to start a puzzle
3. Tap any white cell to select it; tap again to toggle across/down
4. Type letters using the on-screen keyboard
5. Tap "Check" to verify the selected word (wrong letters highlight red)
6. Complete all words to see the victory screen

**No Account Required:**
The app has no login, sign-up, or user accounts.

**No In-App Purchases:**
The app is completely free with no monetization.

**Offline-First:**
All puzzle generation happens on-device. On devices with Apple Intelligence, the app uses the Foundation Models framework for word/clue generation. On other devices, it falls back to a bundled word list. No network requests are made.
