How It Works

Small grids, big fun. Crucigram puzzles are designed to be quick and satisfying.

1

Tap a Cell

Select any cell to see its clue. Tap again to switch between across and down. Use the arrows to navigate between words.

2

Fill the Grid

Type letters to fill in words. The cursor automatically advances to the next empty cell. 5×5 or 6×6 grids keep things quick.

3

Solve & Streak

Complete the puzzle to add to your stats. Solve the daily puzzle every day to build your streak.
